Letting People Braid My Hair

At the end of my junior year, one of my guy friends said that he can braid hair. Because I didn't believe him and I had the easiest hair to braid, he decided to put random braids into my hair. This carried into Senior Year where he would braid my hair or give me a braided rat tail during lunch. During this guys's birthday, as a "fun activity," he and several of my close friends gathered around me and put random braids in my hair. The end result... was exquisitly beautiful...
